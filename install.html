How to Use LetXPath Extension

Please restart your browser to use LetXPath

Step 1: Open LetXPath

  1. Open a Web Page: Navigate to any webpage where you want to use the extension to find XPath expressions.
  2. Activate LetXPath:
    • Click on the LetXPath icon in the Chrome toolbar.
    • This will open the LetXPath panel on the right side of the webpage.

Step 2: Inspect Elements

  1. Select an Element:
    • Click on an element to lock its XPath.
    • The XPath will be shown in the LetXPath panel.

Step 3: Search/Edit XPath

Edit XPath (Optional):

  • You can manually edit the XPath expression in the LetXPath panel.
  • The panel will show the number of matching elements and highlights

Step 4: Copy XPath

  1. Copy the XPath:
    • Click on the Locators expression in the LetXPath panel.
    • This will copy the XPath to your clipboard.

Step 5: Use the snippets

  1. Use in Automation Scripts:
    • Click on the dropdown next to the Locators (e.g., Selenium, Playwright).
    • On selected action snippets copied to clipboard.

Step 6: Axes XPath

  1. Toggle XPath Types:
    • Right click on the webpage to select the Parent element, then the child element
    • You can toggle with different Axes combination

Step 7: Convert XPath to CSS

  1. Open the tools tab:
    • Click the conversion
    • Enter XPath
    • Click Convert
    • Number of matching elements will be shown with highlights

Step 8: Deactivate LetXPath

  1. Close the Panel:
    • Click on the close button on the LetXPath panel or click on the LetXPath icon again to deactivate the extension.

Step 9: Manage LetXPath (Optional)

  1. Extension Settings:
    • Click on the settings icon to choose different framework snippets
